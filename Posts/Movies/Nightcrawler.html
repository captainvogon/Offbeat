Nightcrawler

▣▣▣▣☐

Nightcrawler is a brilliant satire of the media industry and its obsession with violence and sensationalism. It also explores the themes of ambition, morality, and loneliness in a cynical and ruthless world.

The movie is full of suspense, action, and dark humor that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Gyllenhaal delivers a stunning performance as Lou, a character who is both fascinating and terrifying. He transforms himself physically and emotionally to portray a man who has no empathy, no remorse, and no limits.

Nightcrawler is not for the faint of heart, but if you enjoy movies that thrill you, you will love this one. 4/5.
