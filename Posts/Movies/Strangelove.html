Dr. Strangelove

▣▣▣▣▣

Dr. Strangelove is a masterpiece of satire, with brilliant performances, witty dialogue, and absurd scenarios. It's a movie that mocks the madness of the Cold War, the paranoia of the nuclear age, and the incompetence of the people in power. It's a movie that shows how easily the world could end in a catastrophe, and how ridiculous that would be. It's one of the most original, hilarious, and intelligent films ever made.

This movie is a timeless classic. Even after 60 years, the comedy is still hysterical.

"Gentlemen, you can't fight in here! This is the War Room!". 5/5.
