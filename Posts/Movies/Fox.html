Fantastic Mr. Fox

▣▣▣▣▣

Fantastic Mr. Fox is a delight for both kids and adults, as it combines humor and action in the classic Wes Anderson way. The animation style is charming, using stop-motion techniques and hand-made puppets that give the film a distinctive look and feel. The voice cast is superb, with Clooney delivering a charismatic and suave performance as the titular hero, and Streep adding warmth and grace as his wife.

The film is full of witty references and homages to other movies. The soundtrack is also excellent, as is the cinematography.

Fantastic Mr. Fox is the kind of movie you rarely find these days, and is a must watch. 5/5.
