The Grand Budapest Hotel

▣▣▣▣▣

The Grand Budapest Hotel is full of quirky characters, witty dialogue, and stunning visuals. The cast is phenomenal, with Ralph Fiennes as the charming and eccentric Gustave, Tony Revolori as the earnest and loyal Zero, and a host of other stars in supporting roles. The movie is also a tribute to the golden age of cinema, with references to classic films and genres, and a nostalgic style that evokes the past.

This movie is a rare gem that combines humor, drama, romance, and adventure in a perfect balance. It is a movie you will want to watch again and again.

I like posh people talking in fancy english accents. 5/5.
