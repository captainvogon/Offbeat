Pi (1998)

▣▣▣▣☐

Pi is a movie that will keep you on the edge of your seat and make you think about the meaning of life and the nature of reality. It is full of dark humor, surreal imagery, and stunning cinematography. The soundtrack is amazing too, featuring chaotic electronic music that matches the mood of the film perfectly.

This is not a movie for everyone. It is complex, challenging, and sometimes disturbing. But if you're looking for a movie that is unique and different to anything else you've watched before, you should definitely watch Pi (1998).

Darren Aronofsky really took it home with this one. 4/5.
