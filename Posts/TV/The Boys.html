The Boys

▣▣▣▣▣

The Boys is a brilliant satire on the superhero genre, exposing the corruption and hypocrisy of the so-called heroes who are actually controlled by a greedy corporation. The show is full of twists and turns, lots of gore, and some of the best performances you'll ever see on TV.

The show also tackles some relevant social issues, such as racism, sexism, celebrity culture, and media manipulation. Also, Karl Urban is just a complete badass.

The show is not for the faint of heart, but if you can handle it, you'll be watching one of the best TV series ever made. 5/5.
