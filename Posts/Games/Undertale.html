Undertale

▣▣▣▣▣

Undertale is a quirky and charming role-playing game that lets you choose how to interact with the characters and the environment. You can fight, spare, or even flirt with your enemies, and your actions will affect the outcome of the story. The game is full of humor, surprises, and secrets, and will really make you think about your choices.

It breaks the fourth wall, subverts your expectations, and challenges your morality. It also has a catchy soundtrack, retro-style graphics, and a lot of replay value. If you are looking for a game that is original, creative, and fun, then you should definitely give Undertale a try.

It's $5. Just go buy it. 5/5.
